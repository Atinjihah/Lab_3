Campus Highlights: Youth Physical Wellness Program

One of the most valuable experiences was the **Youth Physical Wellness Program** held in **June 2024** during Semester 2. This program emphasizes that health is crucial, even amidst the heavy workload of IT studies. It directly contributed to my **P3 (Guided Respond)** psychomotor learning objective by involving physical activities under supervision.

Group activity during the wellness session.

Team collaboration during the physical challenges.

Skills Developed

Participating in this program helped me practice discipline, time management, and reinforced the importance of balancing academics with physical health, essential skills for any IT professional.
